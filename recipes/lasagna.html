Baked Lasagna

Here you will find an easy recipe to make delicious baked lasagna!

There is nothing quite like hot, fresh, homemade baked lasgana. It is a warm, satisfying, delicious, filling, and inexpensive meal that is sure to please everyone in the family. From the stringy cheese to the delicious beef, this lasgana from this recipe is sure to hit the spot! Simply head to your local grocery store and pick up the following ingredients below to get started.

  • ground beef
  • italian seasoning
  • garlic powder
  • onion powder
  • pepper
  • parmesan cheese
  • marinara sauce
  • ricotta cheese
  • egg
  • mozzarella cheese
  • lasagna noodles
  • fresh basil

Once you have all your ingredients, simply follow the easy step-by-step directions below!

  1. Preheat the oven to 375*F
  2. Spray a 9x13-inch casserole dish with nonstick cooking spray
  3. Brown ground beef over medium heat in a large skillet - season with italian seasoning, garlic powder, onion powder, and black pepper
  4. Drain excess grease
  5. Remove skillet from heat, add in 1/4 cup of parmesan cheese and marinara sauce
  6. Stir all items together, then set aside
  7. In a large bown, combine ricotta cheese, egg, 2 cups shredded mozzarella, and remaining parmesan cheese
  8. Mix all these ingredients until smooth
  9. Cook lasagna noodles according to directions on packaging
  10. Lay cooked noodles separately on a sheet tray, spraying the sheets of noodles lightly with cooking spray too reduce sticking
  11. Begin assembly of lasgana by adding 1 cup of the combined sauce from the skillet to the bottom of the 9x13 casserole dish. Spread it out evenly
  12. Add a layer of 5 lasagna noodles on top, cutting if needed to fit
  13. Top with 1/3 cheese mixture, smoothing it evenly
  14. Add 1 cup of sauce mixture, spreading it out evenly
  15. Repeat the previous 3 steps two more times
  16. Top with the last 5 lasagna noodles and the rest of the sauce
  17. Cover with foil and back for 25 minutes until bubbly
  18. Remove foil and add 2 cups of mozzarella cheese
  19. Place back in oven for 5 minutes or until the cheese is completely melted
  20. Remove from oven and let cool for 15 minutes
  21. Garnish with fresh basil
