Beef Chili

The only recipe you need to make delicious, hearty, homemade chili!

In the mood for a hot, hearty delicious meal that will feed the whole family without breaking the bank? This simple and tasty chili recipe is sure to accompish that and leave you with leftovers! Chili is evem better on the second day. Simply gather the ingredients below and follow the step by step guide and you'll have a perfect meal done before you know it!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion - finely diced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 15-oz can pinto beans
  • 1 15-oz can kidney beans
  • 1 15-oz can diced tomatoes
  • 1 15-oz can tomato sauce
  • 3 tablespoons tomato paste
  • 1 tablespoon maple syrup
  • 3 tablespoons chili powder
  • 2 teaspoons garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 teaspoon smoked paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground pepper
  • 1 cup beef broth

Step by step instructions

With your ingredients all gathered, you can simply follow the steps below to create delicious chili that the whole family will love!

  1. Heat a large stockpot over med/high heat
  2. Add ground beef, yellow onion, minced garlic
  3. Saute for 8 to 10 minutes or until the beef is browned
  4. Add pinto beans, kidney beans,tomato sauce, diced tomatoes, tomato paste, and maple syrup to the pot and stir until combined
  5. Add spices and 1 cup of broth and stir
  6. Bring chili to a boil, then reduce heat to low and let simmer for 15 minutes, or until thickened
  7. Serve in a bowl with your favorite toppings
  8. Enjoy!
